FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started with Best Work Solution.

How quickly can I get a VA?
Most clients are matched with a qualified virtual assistant within 48–72 hours of their initial inquiry. We keep a pre-vetted roster ready to deploy quickly.
What industries do you serve?
We serve clients across e-commerce, real estate, finance, healthcare, marketing agencies, coaches, consultants, startups, and more. Our VAs are trained to adapt to any industry.
Are your VAs trained and experienced?
Absolutely. Every VA on our platform goes through a multi-stage screening process including skills tests, software proficiency checks, and professional interviews before being placed.
What if I'm not satisfied with my VA?
We offer a satisfaction guarantee. If at any point you're not happy with your VA's performance, we will find you a replacement at no additional cost. Your satisfaction is our priority.
What hours do VAs work?
VAs are flexible and can work in your time zone, whether you need part-time (20 hrs/week), full-time (40 hrs/week), or around-the-clock coverage through shift scheduling.
How is billing and payment handled?
We operate on transparent monthly billing with no hidden fees. Packages are tailored to your usage and can be scaled up or down at any time based on your business needs.
Do you offer Web Design & Funnels?
Yes! We now offer full Web Design, Sales Funnel Creation, and Business Automation services. These can be bundled with VA support or hired as standalone digital projects.
What automation tools do you use?
Our automation specialists work with Zapier, Make (formerly Integromat), GoHighLevel, ActiveCampaign, and more — building workflows that connect your tools and eliminate manual work.
