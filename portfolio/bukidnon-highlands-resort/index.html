About the Resort

An Escape Above
the Clouds

Perched at 1,200 meters above sea level in the lush highlands of Bukidnon, Bukidnon Highlands Resort is where modern luxury meets untouched nature. Here, the cool mountain breeze replaces the air conditioning, and the sweeping views replace any screen.

Whether you're seeking a romantic weekend retreat, a family adventure, or a corporate offsite unlike any other — our resort offers an experience rooted in the natural beauty of Mindanao's highlands. Every detail, from the locally crafted interiors to the farm-to-table dining, reflects our commitment to Bukidnon's culture and environment.
