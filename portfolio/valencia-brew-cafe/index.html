Our Story

A Cup Born from
Bukidnon's Highlands

Valencia Brew Café started with a simple belief — that great coffee deserves a great place to be enjoyed. Nestled in the cool highlands of Valencia City, Bukidnon, we source our beans from local farmers right here in the province and roast them to bring out every note of flavor.

Whether you're here for a quiet morning with a book, a catch-up with friends, or a creative work session — we've built this space for you. Every cup is made to order, every detail is considered, and every visit is meant to feel like home.
